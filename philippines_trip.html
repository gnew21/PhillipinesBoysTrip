Itinerary
Stop 01
Moalboal, Cebu
Dec 2 – 6
4 nights
🏡 Bungalow 1: Garden View Room · 3 × Entire Bungalow (2 per bungalow) · Dec 2–6 · Guest: Gabe Newhouse A$189/person ✓
Day 1 · Dec 2
Arrival
Land 5:15pm, van south to Moalboal (~2 hrs, ~A$10). Check in, get food, easy first night.
Day 2 · Dec 3
Explore
Settle in. Moalboal is a small beach town — walk it, find your dive shop for the week, get your bearings. Easy day.
Day 3 · Dec 4
Whale Sharks Scuba
Oslob Whale Sharks + Sardine Run · Island Trek Tours · All-inclusive private group package.

5am pickup → Oslob whale shark dive (1 hr) → Tumalog Falls → Moalboal sardine run + sea turtle dive → back by 7pm. Transport, equipment, breakfast and all fees included.

⚠️ Dec 4 is Christmas week — expect queues at Oslob. Book early and confirm pickup time from Moalboal.
Day 4 · Dec 5
Scuba
Moalboal dive day. Sardines and turtles right off the beach — no boat needed. Some of the best shore diving in Asia.
Day 5 · Dec 6
Adventure
Kawasan Falls — Full Course Canyoneering. Pickup 8–8:30am from accommodation (free for Moalboal/Badian guests). Full route, cliff jumping into electric-blue water, free lunch after.

Afternoon: pack up, van north to Mactan (~2 hrs).
✅ No diving today — safe to fly tomorrow.

1 night

Day 5 Eve · Dec 6
Sightseeing Casino
Check in from 3pm. Visit the Mactan Shrine — where Magellan was killed in 1521. Free, 30 mins. Then dinner and casino night in the hotel — blackjack, drinks. Early night though, flight at 9:20am.
Day 6 · Dec 7
Departure
Airport is 10 mins away. Up at 7am, airport by 7:30am, departs 9:20am to El Nido.

3 nights
🏨 Sea Cocoon Hotel · 3 × Standard Twin Room · Check-in Mon 7 Dec 2pm · Check-out Thu 10 Dec 12pm · 8.7 rating · 9.5 location A$199/person ✓
Day 6 · Dec 7
Arrival
Fly in, check in from 2pm. Walk the town, easy first evening.
Day 7 · Dec 8
Surf
Duli Beach. Hidden jungle beach in north El Nido. Mellow beginner waves, virtually no crowds. All day board hire — A$25/person.
Day 8 · Dec 9
Sightseeing
Bacuit Bay — Shared Island Hopping Tour. 9:30am · 6 hrs · Meet at El Nido town.
Secret Lagoon → Shimizu Island → Seven Commandos Beach → Big Lagoon. Guide and lunch included.

⚠️ Extra cash needed on day: eco-tourism fee 400 PHP (~A$9) + Big Lagoon entry 200 PHP (~A$4.50). No hotel pickup.
Day 9 · Dec 10
Flex Day
Relaxed last day in El Nido. Options:
🤿 El Nido reef dive — local dive shop, good sites around the bay
🏄 More surf at Duli — second session if Day 7 was a hit
🏖️ Do nothing — beach, hammock, cold San Mig
Transfer
Evening — Van to Puerto Princesa. ~5 hrs. Arrive PPS night of Dec 10.
🚐 El Nido → Puerto Princesa · Tourist Minivan · ~5 hrs A$19.45/person ✓
